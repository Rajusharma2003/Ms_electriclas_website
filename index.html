Our FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find comprehensive answers to frequently asked questions to help you gain better clarity and make informed decisions.

1. What services do you offer for switchyard and substation maintenance?

We provide maintenance and testing services for switchyards and substations up to 220 kV, including equipment inspection, fault detection, and performance optimization to ensure reliable operation.

2. How do you handle LT/HT line maintenance?

Our LT/HT line maintenance services include regular inspections, fault identification, repairs, and preventive measures to ensure safe and efficient power transmission.

3. What types of testing do you conduct on transformers, cables, panels, and switchgear?

We perform insulation resistance testing, high-voltage testing, thermal imaging, and other diagnostic tests to assess the health and efficiency of transformers, cables, panels, and switchgear.

4. Why is transformer oil filtration important, and how often should it be done?

Transformer oil filtration removes moisture, sludge, and impurities, enhancing insulation and cooling properties. Regular filtration, typically every 2–3 years, extends transformer life and improves performance.

5. Do you provide maintenance services for solar rooftop systems?

Yes, we offer comprehensive solar rooftop maintenance, including panel cleaning, performance checks, wiring inspections, and fault troubleshooting to maximize energy efficiency and system lifespan.
